Bulk Student Score Prediction

Upload a dataset to predict exam scores for multiple students.


Required Dataset Columns

Your dataset should contain the following fields:

  • study_hours_per_day
  • attendance_percentage
  • screen_time
  • entertainment_hours
  • stress_level
  • exam_anxiety
  • previous_gpa
  • motivation_level
  • major (optional)

Notes

  • Previous GPA can be entered on a 0–10 scale. The system will automatically convert it to the 0–4 scale used during training.
  • If your dataset contains social_media_hours and netflix_hours, the system will automatically combine them into entertainment_hours.
  • The output dataset will contain a new column called predicted_exam_score.
